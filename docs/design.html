Indigo Variation · Dark Mode

Indigo

Quiet confidence in deep space

A design system for creative instruments that work at the speed of thought. Deep indigo surfaces provide a stage where content can breathe. Layered shadows give elements material presence. Generous negative space creates rhythm. Every interaction carries weight without heaviness — tactile, precise, emotionally resonant.

Philosophy

Design as atmosphere. Interface as instrument. The tool should feel like an extension of creative intent, not a barrier to it. Deep indigo creates a nocturnal studio — calm, focused, infinite.

Typography Stack

Cormorant Garamond for display and headings. Manrope for body. IBM Plex Mono for labels, metadata, and structural notation.

01 / Surface & Material

The surface breathes. Light arrives from above.

Cards use linear gradient backgrounds moving from surface to surface-strong, creating subtle depth. Multi-layer shadows include an inset highlight at the top edge — simulating light falling on a physical material. A grain texture overlay at low opacity prevents digital flatness.

Grain Texture

SVG noise at 7% opacity across the viewport. Creates analog film quality that prevents the interface from feeling sterile.

Ambient Lighting

Radial gradients positioned at top-left (cool blue), top-right (warm highlight), and bottom (dark vignette). Simulates environmental light.

Inset Highlights

Every elevated surface carries a 1px top inset with 3–5% white opacity. This catches the ambient light and gives cards their material edge.

Material Presence

Every surface has weight. Gradients simulate depth, inset highlights catch ambient light, and multi-layer shadows anchor elements to the canvas. Nothing floats without reason. The interface has the tactile quality of a well-made instrument.

Emotional Spacing

Spacing is not just functional — it carries tone. Tight gaps create intimacy between related elements. Breathing room between sections creates calm. Distant landmarks give the eye places to rest. The rhythm is musical, not mechanical.

Quiet Confidence

The interface never shouts. Accent colors appear at reduced opacity. Animations are swift and purposeful. Labels are small, uppercase, spaced — they orient without demanding attention. Confidence is in the restraint, not the volume.
